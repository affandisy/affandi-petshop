About Us

Our company specializes in providing professional pet care services, including grooming, veterinary clinics, and exclusive pet hotels. Supported by experienced professionals and modern facilities, we are committed to delivering the best care that is safe, comfortable, and reliable for every pet.

Vision

To become the leading and most trusted provider of pet care services in Indonesia with international standards, prioritizing professionalism and comfortable.

Mission

  1. To provide high-quality grooming, veterinary, and pet hotel services that are safe and comfortable.
  2. To prioritize the health and well-being of pets through professional experts and modern facilities.
  3. To create a friendly, hygienic, and compassionate environment for every pet.
